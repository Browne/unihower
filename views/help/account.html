Your account

Create your account

How to

Thanks for trying out Eisenhower - we think you'll really love it!

To get started click, from any page click Login with Persona

Enter the email address you would like to sign-up with and press next. If you already have a Persona account enter your password and you're done!

If you have never used Persona before, enter an email address, create a password and click the link in the confirmation email.

Did you know: You can sign into other services with Persona.

You'll then be taken direct to your new users page, where you can optionally enter your name. From there, agree to the terms and conditions then click .

Troubleshooting

Make sure you enter the correct email address when you login the first time. If you don't you can just go back and start again!

If Persona won't let you login, double check you have clicked the link in the email.

Forgotten your password? Follow the instructions on the Login screen.

Checking it works

When you've got an account, you're name will show in the top-right corner of the screen, if you didn't set one, then your email address will appear.

Modify your account

How to

First, navigate to either the task list or topic that contains the task you wish to modify. Click on the task you wish to modify.

Click Edit Task then change the fields you wish to change. From this view, you can also change the status of a task to complete.

To finish, press

Did you know: To complete a task, just click Mark Complete instead of editing the task.

Troubleshooting

Make sure you have navigated to the correct task and clicked Edit Task

Also make sure you don't leave the name field blank.

Checking it works

You can check in the tasks list to make sure the task has been updated

If you changed the status of the task, the pictogram should change colour and icon.

Did you know: A green circle with a tick is a task marked as complete. An orange circle with a cross is in progress.

Your account

Danger Delete your account

How to

Note: This action is irreversible

First navigate to the tasks list. Alternatively, navigate to the topic containing the task you want to delete.

Click on the task you wish to delete.

Double check you are about to delete the correct task, click Delete Task and accept the confirmation message.

Troubleshooting

Make sure you navigate to the correct task.

If you have deleted a task by mistake, unfortunately it cannot be recovered.

Checking it works

You will automatically be taken back to your topic page and the task should no longer appear
